Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of events do you staff?

We staff all kinds of events, from weddings and corporate parties to private dinners, festivals, and more. Whether you need servers, bartenders, or full event support, we’ve got you covered!

How does booking your staff work?

It’s simple! Tell us about your event, and we’ll match you with the right team based on your needs. We handle everything—scheduling, coordination, and making sure our staff shows up ready to go.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on the type of staff you need, the number of hours, and the event size. Get in touch with us for a custom quote—no hidden fees, just straightforward pricing.

Do your staff wear uniforms?

Yes! Our team can wear classic black-and-white attire, branded shirts, or even themed outfits to match your event.

Is there a minimum booking time?

Yes, we have a minimum booking time of three hours to ensure smooth service and a great experience for your guests.
